    All you want to know about us

    We practice of raising domesticated birds, such as chickens, ducks, turkeys, and geese, for the purpose of producing meat, eggs, and sometimes feathers

    Wr practice of raising domesticated birds, such as chickens, ducks, turkeys, and geese, for the purpose of producing meat, eggs, and sometimes feathers. Poultry farming is a significant branch of agriculture and plays a crucial role in providing protein-rich food products to a global population.

    • Regular check-ups and access to veterinary care to address health issues
    • Breeders may use selective breeding to enhance desirable traits such as productivity and disease resistance.
    • Eggs are either allowed to be naturally incubated by broody hens or are placed in artificial incubators

    Poultry farming can take various forms, from small backyard operations to large commercial enterprises. It requires a combination of knowledge, management skills, and a commitment to animal welfare to be successful. Additionally, advancements in technology and agricultural practices continue to influence and improve the efficiency of poultry farming
